## Scope of Work

These papers span several recurring directions in my research: topology in kagome and magnetic materials, symmetry breaking in charge-ordered systems,
surface-bulk disentangling in ARPES, and hybrid or higher-order topological responses in real materials.

## Other Publications

Beyond the first-author and co-first-author papers above, my collaborative work includes spectroscopy studies of Weyl physics,
Kondo-lattice materials, and related topological quantum matter systems.
