## Selected Projects

### DrawBZ

DrawBZ is a lightweight Python project for constructing bulk and surface Brillouin zones for complex crystal structures.
It grew out of day-to-day research needs and is meant to reduce repetitive geometry work in condensed matter workflows.

- Related post: <a href="/posts/2022/07/blog-post-1/">Drawing BZ without Any Pain</a>
- Code: <a href="https://github.com/Zijia-Cheng/DrawBZ">GitHub repository</a>

### Quantitative research interests

Beyond academic physics, I am interested in statistical modeling, machine learning, and quantitative research workflows.
That includes building interpretable models, stress-testing signals, and treating noisy real-world data with the same discipline used in physics.
